About Me

I have been photographing weddings, elopements, and family portraits in the Niagara region for over 14 years now. I love working with couples and families from all over the area, and those visiting Niagara Falls as well! There are so many beautiful backdrops around the Niagara region for your photography needs. I find it's important to plan out your shoot in advance, so we can get the best possible photos for you. I can offer advice on clothing to wear, or different locations to use.
