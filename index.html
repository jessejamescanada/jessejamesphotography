Who I am

I have been photographing weddings, elopements, family portraits, maternity and proposals for over 13 years now. Originally from Niagara Falls, Canada, I moved to Chicago at the end of 2020. I love working with couples and families from all over the area, and those visiting Chicago as well! There are so many beautiful backdrops around the Chicago area for your photography needs. I find it's important to plan out your shoot in advance, so we can get the best possible photos for you. I can offer advice on clothing to wear, or different locations to use.

Services

Chicago Elopement Photography

Elopements are my favorite to photograph. I love the intimacy of a small elopement. Just focusing on the couple and (sometimes) a few close friends and family produces some fantastic moments. Although my focus is more on elopement photography, I also offer wedding coverage and have photographed over 400 weddings in my career.

Chicago Family Photography

Family photos are such an important keepsake to have. There's some beautiful areas around Chicago we can use for the backdrop to your family portraits. I like to mix things up with some classic poses and then also catch the candid moments in between. I also offer senior portraits and maternity photos.

Chicago Engagement Photography

Photographing engagements is so much fun! You get dressed up, we go to cool locations, things are laid back and we capture some awesome memories!
