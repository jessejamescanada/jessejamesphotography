Who I am

I have been photographing weddings, elopements, family portraits, maternity and proposals in the Niagara region for over 12 years now. I love working with couples and families from all over the area, and those visiting Niagara Falls as well! There are so many beautiful backdrops around the Niagara region for your photography needs. I find it's important to plan out your shoot in advance, so we can get the best possible photos for you. I can offer advice on clothing to wear, or different locations to use. Check out my blog to see photos from some of my most recent shoots. Blog

Services

Niagara Weddings

I have photographed over 400 weddings around Niagara over the past 12 years. I will still be taking on a few larger weddings for 2020. Please contact me to discuss your wedding photography needs.

Niagara Family

Family photos are such an important keepsake to have. There's some beautiful areas around Niagara we can use for the backdrop to your family portraits. And for those visiting Niagara Falls I have a custom family package available now with the falls in the background!

Niagara Elopements

Niagara Falls and elopements go hand in hand. The intimacy of a small wedding can't be matched. And getting to meet so many couples who are visiting Niagara Falls for their destination wedding is fantastic. We can use the falls as a backdrop, or go to one of the many gorgeous parks that Niagara has to offer for your photos.

Niagara Maternity

Some of my favourite shoots are maternity photos. Capturing the beauty of your pregnancy either as a solo portrait or with your significant other is a great way to capture such an awesome milestone in your life.
