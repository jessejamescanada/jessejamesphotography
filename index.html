Your Niagara Wedding Photographer

2026 marks the 17th year I have been a Niagara Wedding Photographer! I've been lucky enough to photograph over 450 weddings! Clients often describe me as friendly and calm which I find helps my couples relax on their big day. My experience has helped me navigate all of the different unexpected scenarios that may arise on your wedding day. My goal is to capture your one of the most important moments of your life in a way that will bring a smile to your face every time you look back on your photos.

My work has been featured on numerous wedding websites, magazine covers and billboards around Niagara.

In my role as your Niagara wedding or elopement photographer, my main aim is to ensure that your photos tell a compelling story with top-notch quality. I want your pictures to strike a balance between unique and timeless and have your friends and family say "wow!" when they see them.

Besides photography, I am a huge animal lover. I just got a puppy named Teddy! I love to be outside hiking or golfing and in my spare time I code websites.

Also, I love photographing LGBTQ+ weddings. All love stories are welcome here.
