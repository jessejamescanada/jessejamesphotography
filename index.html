About Me

I've been lucky enough to photograph over 450 weddings around Niagara and Hamilton since 2009! Clients often describe me as friendly and calm which I find helps my couples relax on their big day. My experience has helped me navigate all of the different unexpected scenarios that may arise on your wedding day. From limos breaking down and having to drive the couple myself, unexpected fires (yes, really), bad weather and many other events. I'll always make sure you get the best images on your wedding day.

My work has been featured on numerous wedding websites, magazine covers and billboards around Niagara.

In my role as your wedding or family photographer, my main aim is to ensure that your photos tell a compelling story with top-notch quality. I want your pictures to strike a balance between unique and timeless and have your friends and family say "wow!" when they see them.

Besides photography, I am a huge animal lover. I just got a puppy named Teddy! I love to be outside hiking or golfing and in my spare time I code websites.

Shoot me a message in the form below and lets discuss your photography needs :)
