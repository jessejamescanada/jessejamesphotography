About Me

I have been photographing weddings, elopements, and family portraits in the Niagara and Hamilton regions of Southern Ontario for over 14 years now. I love working with couples and families from all over the area, and those visiting Niagara Falls as well! I find it's important to plan out your shoot in advance, so we can get the best possible photos for you. I can offer advice on clothing to wear, or different locations to use.
