Niagara Elopement Photography

What is an Elopement?

Do you want to get married but not have all the stress and expenses that come along with a traditional big wedding? Then getting eloped might be right for you! I've photographed elopements that have anywhere from 2 to 20 guests in total, and I've shot elopements where it's just the bride and groom and I get to be one of the witnesses who signs the marriage license! An elopement is a great way to celebrate with your closest family and friends and eliminate a lot of the stress and planning.

Why elope in Niagara?

The Niagara region has always been a popular spot for weddings, Niagara Falls itself is known as the honeymoon capital of the world! And in the past few years elopements have really gained popularity with couples who want to get married but want to keep it small and intimate.

Niagara Falls and Niagara on the Lake have become popular vacation destination spots for couples who want to get married. there are so many locations to choose from. Some couples like to get eloped right in front of Niagara Falls and have the waterfall as a backdrop. Others use some of the beautiful parks like the Oakes Garden Theatre or the Botanical Gardens, and others even have their elopement ceremony on the Hornblower boat while Niagara Falls is roaring just a few hundred feet away. And then historic Niagara on the Lake offers beautiful parks, vintage hotels and some of the most beautiful wineries and vineyards in all of Canada.


Planning an Elopement in Niagara?

Often times I end up being the first person a couple contacts when they are planning to get eloped. I've photographed well over 100 elopements around Niagara and I will gladly give you various tips I've learned throughout the years. I also can give you recommendations on different locations around Niagara to have your ceremony or where we should go for photos after, and I can give you recommendations on different vendors from ministers, flowers, makeup & hair, and restaurants to have a small reception or dinner after.
