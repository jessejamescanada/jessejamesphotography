Niagara Family Photography

I love love love family photography. The memories we can create and capture are timeless. I love delivering photos that you will cherish for forever. And the Niagara region has so many beautiful backdrops for family photos! I always capture different poses for group shots and then break it down into smaller groups and individuals of the kids, etc! I do give posing suggestions as were shooting but I always make sure to keep it feeling natural for you. I also offer maternity photography and you are always welcome to bring your pets do the shoot as well. After all, they're family too!

Family photography packages start at $299 and come with the fully edited files in both colour and black and white of each shot.


Niagara Falls Family Package


Over the years I have been asked by a lot of tourists about photographing their families while they are visiting Niagara Falls, with the waterfall as a backdrop for some of the photos. These shoots are so much fun! Please contact me for a custom Niagara Falls Family package.
