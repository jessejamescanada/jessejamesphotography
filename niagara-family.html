Niagara Family Photographer

I love love love family photography. The memories we can create and capture are timeless. I love delivering photos that you will cherish for forever. And the Niagara region has so many beautiful backdrops for family photos! I always capture different poses for group shots and then break it down into smaller groups and individuals of the kids, etc! I do give posing suggestions as were shooting but I always make sure to keep it feeling natural for you. I also offer maternity photography and you are always welcome to bring your pets do the shoot as well. After all, they're family too!

Niagara Falls Family Packages


The most popular family package is $425 and includes a one hour shoot, up to 5 people, 50+ (there is no limit) fully edited high resolution photos in BOTH colour and black and white plus an online gallery. I also offer a shorter 30 minute package for $299 which includes 20 fully edited photos.

Over the years I have been asked by a lot of people visiting Niagara Falls about photographing their families here with the waterfall as a backdrop for some of the photos. These shoots are so much fun! Please contact me for a custom Family package.
