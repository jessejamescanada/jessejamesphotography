Wedding Photography

* Check Out 2023 Niagara Wedding Photography Year in Review *

Weddings in Niagara. Talk about the perfect combination. With beautiful parks, beaches, the vineyards in Niagara on the Lake, vintage hotels, large banquet halls and lets not forget the beauty of Niagara Falls and the giant waterfall, there is a spot here that fits any style of wedding that you want. It's no wonder that people travel from all over the world to get married in the beautiful Niagara region. Throughout my career I have been lucky enough to capture over 450 of those weddings! I also shoot weddings in and around Hamilton and I'm always willing to travel as well!


Wedding photography has been my main focus since I started my business in 2009. I believe it is SO important for the couple to have the right photographer to capture their wedding day. I like to meet with potential couples for an initial consultation to make sure our personalities match. I always strive to keep my couples relaxed and feeling comfortable. I provide gentle direction and put you in different scenarios ("Hold hands, try to make each other laugh and walk towards me") so your photos have a natural and genuine look to them. I realize not everyone is comfortable in front of the camera (me included!) and I find this way works best. I would describe my style as a mix of timeless, traditional photos mixed with a modern and fun flair.

Your wedding photos will be delivered within 3-6 weeks. There is no limit to how many you receive. Your photos come fully edited, in high resolution, and I include both a colour and black and white version of each photo. They're put into an online gallery to make it easy to share with family and friends. And that gallery doesn't expire, ever.
