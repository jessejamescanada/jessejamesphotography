Niagara Wedding Photographer

Weddings in Niagara. Talk about the perfect combination. People travel from all over the world to get married in the beautiful Niagara region, and I have been lucky enough to capture over 400 of those weddings in my career!


Wedding photography has been my main focus since I started in 2009. I believe it is SO important for the couple to have the right photographer to capture their wedding day. I always strive to keep my couples relaxed and feeling good. I give gentle direction to you, your family and your wedding party so you still look and feel natural. I always deliver a mix of the timeless traditional photos and a more modern and candid style. I also photograph weddings outside of Niagara. As a dual citizen I also photograph weddings in the United States occasionally and have shot weddings in Chicago, Charleston and West Virginia.


Niagara Wedding Photography Packages

I offer multiple wedding packages, starting at $1899 and going up from there. All photography packages come with the fully edited high resolution files in colour AND black and white of each photo, an online gallery to download everything from, and options to add an engagement shoot or a second shooter. All packages can be customized. Please contact me for further details.

Winter Weddings

Niagara is beautiful during every season! I love winter weddings. Yes it's cold but the snow looks gorgeous for your photos! I do offer a small discount on winter wedding photography. Please be sure to contact me for more details

Engagement / Couples Photos

I also offer standalone engagement and couple portrait sessions. Niagara has a lot of visitors each year and I am always glad to capture your engagement or couples photos! The package is $399, and comes with 2 locations, an outfit change, and the fully edited high resolution files in both colour and black and white of each.
