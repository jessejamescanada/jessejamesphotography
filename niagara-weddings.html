Niagara Wedding Photography

Weddings in Niagara. Talk about the perfect combination. People travel from all over the world to get married in the beautiful Niagara region, and I have been lucky enough to capture over 400 of those weddings in my career!


Wedding photography has been my main focus since I started in 2009. I believe it is SO important for the couple to have the right photographer to capture their wedding day. I always strive to keep my couples relaxed and feeling good. I give gentle direction to you, your family and your wedding party so you still look and feel natural. I always deliver a mix of the timeless traditional photos and a more modern and candid style.


The best part about elopements in Niagara is that there are so many locations to choose from. Some people like to get eloped right in front of Niagara Falls and have the waterfall as a backdrop. But there are also wineries in Niagara on the Lake, Niagara Parks offers some beautiful locations, and I have even done some in peoples backyards.

Niagara Wedding Photography Packages

All packages can be customized. Please contact me for further details.
