Mommy and Me Tutu Shoot | Niagara on the Lake

I love this beautiful tradition that Alesha shares with her daughter Quinn. Every year the two of them get dressed up in matching tutu's for a mommy-and-me family session. This year we chose Niagara on the Lake as the location and the results were awesome!


I met the two of them right downtown in front of the Prince of Wales hotel. The two of them were getting so many compliments and curious questions as to why they were hanging out in tutu's on a busy summer evening in Niagara on the Lake. Our session began on the busy main strip and then we headed off to the unique side streets that Niagara on the Lake offers.


This was my 4th time doing a tutu session with them and as usual they were fantastic to work with. Alesha is also a talented family / wedding photographer in Niagara and she has Quinn loving getting her photo taken. We found some beautiful and unique locations to use and the two of them are naturals in front of the camera.
