Dunnville Farm Family Portraits

10 kids, 2 sets of grandparents, a farm in Dunnville and 4 mini family shoots. What a fun evening that was with Chelsie and her family recently.


Working with the kids was a delight; they were all cooperative and brought their unique personalities to the photoshoot. The farm offered a picturesque backdrop, enhancing the beauty of their family portraits. We began with group photos, capturing the love and togetherness of the entire family. As the session progressed, we focused on individual families, and it was a great to see how close all the families are.


I especially loved capturing the candids in-between the posed photos. There was so many opportunities to catch them all with genuine laughs which are some of my favourite to capture.
