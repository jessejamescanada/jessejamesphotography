Waverly Beach Family Portraits

I love family sessions and nothing beats family portraits at the beach. Andrew contacted me a few months ago asking about family portraits. He let me know there would be some family visiting this summer and they wanted to do a multi-family portrait session while everyone was here. The multi-family sessions has been a trend this summer and I was excited to get to work with everyone! After discussing a few different locations around Niagara, we decided to use Waverly Beach for their photos.


I've known Andrew since highschool and it was great to catch up and see how his family has grown. This was a family shoot that was just full of laughs and jokes. The kids were all up for everything I asked of them, and we broke the photos down from large group portraits, individual families and then other groupings and couples shots after that.
