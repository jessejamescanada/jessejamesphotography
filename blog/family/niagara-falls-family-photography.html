Niagara Falls Botanical Gardens Family Photography

I adore this family so much. I first met Star and Rich 12 years ago when they booked me to photograph their engagements at Niagara Falls. Since then I have had the opportunity to photograph their wedding and photograph their family throughout the years. They are some of the nicest people I have ever met, and I am so thankful that photography brought us together and that we are friends.


We last did family photos just before Covid happened, so it was time to update them especially with their son Dylan growing so quickly! For this years family pics, we met at the Botanical Gardens in Niagara Falls. I swear Star and Rich still look the same as they did when I first met them 12 years ago, but their son Dylan has doubled in size since our last shoot! We ended up shooting for over an hour and I think Dylan had us laughing for almost the entire time.


It is always a joy to work with them and see how much love they have for one another. And what a perfect early June evening we had for their photos!
