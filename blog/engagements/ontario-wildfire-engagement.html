Ontario Wildfire Engagements | Kehoe & Kin

You've probably seen it on the news or noticed outside that there are wildfires in Quebec and Northern Ontario and how they're affecting Southern Ontario and most of the eastern part of the continent. The smoke from the fires has made the air hazy and hard to breathe and most of the time it smells like a campfire outside. But even with these conditions, Logan and Colton were troopers and we did their engagement shoot. We met up at Kehoe & Kin, which is also where they're going to have their wedding in a few months. It was my first time there and seriously, the venue is gorgeous. It has this rustic barn vibe and it's hidden in the town of Wainfleet. I took them around the venue to different areas and the two of them were amazing to photograph. It's so obvious how happy they are together and how much love they share. Plus, they both look like models. After we were done at Kehoe & Kin, we decided to go to Morgan's Point in Wainfleet for some more engagement photos. With being on the beach, you could really see how the sky has changed with all the smoke from the wildfires in the air. It gave us some amazing colours and interesting backdrops.


I can't wait for their wedding in September of this year, I know it's going to be a blast!
