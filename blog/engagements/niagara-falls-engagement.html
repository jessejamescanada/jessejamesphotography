Niagara Falls Engagement | Kristy and Luis

Kristy and Luis recently reached out to me. They said they were coming from New Jersey to Niagara Falls for a vacation and wanted to take their engagement photos here. I love getting to photograph couples from all over the world who come to visit Niagara Falls. We picked a date and planned to start at the Botanical Gardens before heading to the Bridal Falls and Horseshoe Falls for some awesome backgrounds.


Kristy and Luis were a lot of fun to photograph, and it was a blast capturing their love. The air had a hazy look to it due to wildfires, but it actually made the lighting look great in the photos. The photos at the rose garden were some of my favourites. And I loved that they both wore Converse Sneakers, so I made sure to get a ring shot featuring their shoes. The two of them were naturals in front of the camera and I noticed that when we were down near the Niagara Falls people couldn't help but stop and watch as I photographed them.


Congrats, you two!
