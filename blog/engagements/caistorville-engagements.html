Caistorville Engagements | Nicole and Kevin

Recently I had the opportunity to photograph Nicole and Kevin's engagements in Caistorville, Ontario. The setting was a picturesque country-style backdrop on a gorgeous summer evening. To add to that, they brought along their two Doberman's and Kevin's race car!


Meeting Nicole and Kevin for the first time was an absolute delight. Their warm and friendly personalities came through in the photos. They were laughing and smiling throughout the entire shoot showcasing the love and happiness they share as a couple.


I'm so excited to photograph their wedding later this summer!
