Niagara Falls Elopement | Damaris and Denzel

One of the best parts about being a photographer in the Niagara region is having the opportunity to photography couples from all over the world who come to Niagara Falls for their elopement. It's such an iconic spot and the waterfall is a beautiful backdrop for a small wedding.


Damaris and Denzel contacted me a couple of months ago. They let me know they would be having a destination wedding ceremony at Niagara Falls City Hall and then wanted to have their photos down by the Falls. We did an early morning shoot by Niagara Falls before it got too busy and the light coming through was perfect for photos.


Damaris and Denzel were the cutest couple. The laughs and smiles they shared throughout their portraits was awesome to capture. It was their first time ever visiting Niagara Falls and it was a joy to see their reaction when they first saw the waterfalls. Getting to know them a little during the shoot was great, and learning that they are huge dog lovers like myself just made me like them even more!


Congratulations you two!
