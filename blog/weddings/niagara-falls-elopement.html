Niagara Falls Elopement

I love photographing elopements at Niagara Falls. There's something about the actual waterfall in the background, the laid back atmosphere of a small elopement, and all the beautiful scenery around Niagara Falls that just cant be beat.


Kate and Brad contacted me in the early fall. They told me they were coming to Niagara Falls all the way from West Virginia and they were planning to elope. We discussed different details about where was best to have their ceremony and the best time of day to have it for their photos. They decided on the 1 hour elopement package that I offer and everything was all set!


The weather for their October elopement was perfect. Decently warm and sunny. The two of them were a joy to work with and it was obvious the two of them are perfect for each other. They didn't have a wedding party so I even got to be the witness and sign their wedding certificate.

Congratulations you two!
