2023 Niagara Wedding Year in Review

Wow, what a year it was photographing such beautiful weddings here in Niagara! From the heartfelt vows to the amazing dance floor moves, 2023 was an absolute blast for wedding photography. Reflecting back, each wedding had its own unique story to tell, making it a year filled with beautiful tales of love and commitment.

Niagara has some of the most stunning backdrops for wedding photography. From the misty falls to the elegant vineyards, it always brings me such joy to capture these milestones and create art with such beautiful people.
Having the opportunity to capture those raw and genuine moments for my couples is something I will always cherish and I would just like to say thank you to everyone I worked with.
Below are a few favourites from some of the weddings I photographed in Niagara this year. Some have links in their description to other blog posts where you can see more photos from that wedding.

2024 Niagara Wedding Photography

2024 is shaping up to be another amazing year capturing weddings and I cannot wait! If you are interested in the wedding photography packages I offer, please head over to my wedding photography page for more information and shoot me a message if you have any questions or would like to chat about your wedding day!


Sugarloaf Marina

What a gorgeous wedding this was between Chelsea and Adam. They had their ceremony at Sugarloaf Marina in Port Colborne and their reception at the Italian Hall. The two of them are some of the kindest and sweetest people I have ever met. Check out the rest of their wedding photos from Sugarloaf Marina.

Kehoe and Kin

Logan and Colton had a stunning rustic barn wedding at Kehoe and Kin in Wainfleet. Their wedding looked straight out of a magazine and their day was filled with lots of love and laughs.

Pillar and Post Summer

Julie and Brian had a beautiful mid summer wedding at Pillar and Post in Niagara on the Lake. As they were two families blending into one, they had their kids as the wedding party. Loved that idea!

White Oaks

Jen and Dan had a stunning summer wedding at White Oaks in Niagara on the Lake. Every single person at their wedding traveled here for it and all of their families and friends couldn't have been more nice if they tried.

Caistorville Wedding

Nicole and Kevin threw a gorgeous wedding in Caistorville I love how they involved their dogs and even had the pups walk down the isle with them after their ceremony!

13th Street Winery

Dogs and weddings were a bit of a theme this year and Jason and Natasha included theirs during their small wedding at 13th Street Winery. Their dogs even stood up front with them during their outdoor ceremony.

Morningstar Mill Elopement

Crystal and Brenden kicked off my wedding year when they had a small elopement and then photos at Morningstar Mill. It was a gorgeous spring day and the two of them looked so happy together.

Pink Cadillac Wedding

Paula and John had their wedding on their property in Fort Erie. It was a joy to capture their day and use John's 1957 Pink Cadillac that his grandfather originally bought brand new.

Pillar and Post

Cindy and Robb finished my summer off with their wedding at Pillar and Post in Niagara on the Lake. I love how colourful and vibrant the gardens at Pillar and Post were for their wedding photos.

Port Colborne Wedding

A beautiful an intimate wedding held at a cottage in Port Colborne. Loved the Mehndi body art and how colourful everything was. After their ceremony they changed outfits and had a great party throughout the night!

Niagara Falls Elopements

I love photographing elopements in Niagara Falls and this year did not disappoint. I had some amazing couples from all over the world who came to Niagara Falls to get married. It was hard to choose a couple to showcase, but here are a couple of favourites from this year.

Harley Davidson Wedding

Such a fun and unique shoot! Bridgette and James got married in Jamaica, but then decided they wanted wedding photos with their Harley Davidson's since their bikes are a big part of their lives. Loved how these turned out!

John Michaels Wedding

Kristy and Paul are a beautiful couple and Paul had Kristy and everyone else at their wedding laughing the entire time. They had a gorgeous outdoor ceremony and indoor reception at John Michaels Banquet Centre.

Stonemill Inn Wedding

An awesome Star Wars themed wedding at Stonemill Inn in St Catharines closed out my wedding season. Meaghan and Todd are such a kind couple and I love how they were able to include their love of Star Wars with their wedding.
