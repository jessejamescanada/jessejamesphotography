Kehoe & Kin Wedding | Logan and Colton

Nestled in the quaint town of Wainfleet, Ontario, Logan and Colton's autumn wedding at Kehoe & Kin was a beautiful celebration of their love.


The day began with Logan and her bridesmaids excitedly getting ready for the wedding. I started by taking photos of the details and then focused on Logan as she got ready. She looked absolutely stunning, like she had walked straight out of a bridal magazine. Her dress, veil, and those captivating blue eyes of hers were simply perfect.


One of the sweetest moments before the wedding was when Logan had her first look with her bridesmaids and her father. There was a lot of emotion in the air, and it was evident how close they all are. The reactions were priceless and their flower girl Juliette couldn't wait to see Logan all dressed up.


The ceremony took place just behind the barn at Kehoe & Kin by the pond. The bridesmaids and groomsmen walked down the aisle with grace and style. When Logan made her entrance, I couldn't help but sneak a peek at Colton. He had tears in his eyes and the most heartwarming smile. It was a beautiful moment.


The wedding ceremony was a heartfelt exchange of vows, filled with loving looks and sincere promises. After Logan and Colton shared their first kiss as a married couple and walked down the aisle, they were showered in cheers and confetti.


After the ceremony, it was time for some fun with the bridal party. I knew they would be a fun group and they didn't disappoint. They were up for everything and each photo captured their joy.


But the most meaningful moments were those shared between Logan and Colton. Their love was evident in every photograph. It was clear that they are meant for each other. The looks they shared, the little whispers between each other, the way each of them lights up when they see each other. They made my job as a wedding photographer extremely easy.


As the reception was about to begin we moved inside the beautifully decorated barn at Kehoe & Kin, the atmosphere exuded warmth and charm.


The evening was a mix of delicious food, heartfelt speeches, and lively dancing. Logan and Colton's autumn wedding was a celebration of love, beauty, and pure happiness. It was a day filled with unforgettable moments, a testament to their love and the promise of a lifetime of joy together.
