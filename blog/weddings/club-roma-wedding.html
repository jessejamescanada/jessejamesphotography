Club Roma Wedding | Amy and Adam

I love photographing weddings at Club Roma, and Amy & Adam's was one of my favourites! They had a gorgeous late spring wedding. Their ceremony was held at the outdoor gardens at Club Roma and it was beautiful. When Adam saw Amy walking down the isle with her dad he couldn't hold back the tears. She looked stunning!


Their families and friends were all so much fun to hang out with. We captured some amazing images and memories throughout their wedding and finished it up with a beautiful reception. Congratulations guys!
