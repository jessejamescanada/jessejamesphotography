Club Roma Wedding | Amy and Adam

I love photographing weddings at Club Roma, and Amy & Adam's was one of my favourites! They had a gorgeous late spring wedding. Their ceremony was held at the outdoor gardens at Club Roma and it was beautiful. When Adam saw Amy walking down the isle with her dad he couldn't hold back the tears. She looked stunning!


After their wedding ceremony we stuck around Club Roma and used their gardens for the wedding party photos. My favourite part of their wedding day was when it was just Amy and Adam for the photos. The two of them are so in love and the two of them make for a very good looking bride and groom!

The reception at Club Roma was decorated beautifully. Amy and Adam shared a beautiful first dance and their parent dances were emotional and perfect. The night finished off with some delicious food, great speeches and a fantastic party afterwards!

Photographing weddings and families in the Niagara and Hamilton regions since 2009!
