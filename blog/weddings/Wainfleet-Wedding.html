Wainfleet Wedding | Courtney and Jacob

What a beautiful late summer backyard wedding Courtney and Jacob had in Wainfleet. Their place was decorated to perfection, they had a mobile mixer for everyone wanting drinks, their family and friends couldn't have been nicer, and the two of them look like they came straight out of a magazine. As a wedding photographer, this wedding was a dream to photograph!


Their wedding day was calling for a chance of rain but besides literally a couple drops, it held off for them. There was some special moments shared between them, I especially loved before their ceremony when they stood on either side of a doorway and shared their feelings for one another. Courtney, Jacob and myself headed out to Morgan's Point in Wainfleet for some wedding portraits of just the two of them, then we returned back to their place for family and wedding party photos, and then some amazing food and an awesome reception.


Congratulations you two!
