Winter Wedding at the Delphi in Niagara Falls

Wow! What an awesome winter wedding here in Niagara. Lydia and Phil contacted me this past summer about photographing their wedding just before Christmas this year at the Delphi in Niagara Falls. When they booked me for a winter wedding, I was hoping we would get some beautiful snow but I wasn't sure if the weather would cooperate with us or not. Thankfully, the weather more than cooperated for us! We got big fluffy flakes that just looked gorgeous for their wedding photos.


I started the day off in Port Colborne at Lydia's parents house. Everyone there was so laid back and their house was decorated beautifully for Christmas. After a sweet and intimate ceremony, we headed to the Port Colborne Museum for their winter wedding photos. It was cold out but Lydia, Phil and their awesome wedding party were troopers and handled the weather like pros. At one point I even got them to have a snowball fight!


After their little winter wonderland of wedding photos we headed over to the Delphi in Niagara Falls which was decorated beautifully for a Christmas wedding!


Congratulations you two :)
