Chelsea and Adam | Italian Hall Wedding

When I think of Chelsea and Adam, a bunch of words come to mind. They're sweet, kind, caringâ€”basically the definition of awesome parents. They deserved to have an amazing wedding day and it truly ended up being a fantastic day. I started the day off hanging with Adam and the guys as they geared up for the big event. We had a blast with tons of laughs.


Then I swung by the salon where Chelsea and her bridesmaids were getting ready. I have to say, Chelsea was an absolute knockout. She looked like she came straight out of a bridal magazine. And I love that we got a photo of her daughter wearing Chelsea's wedding dress. It was too cute for words.


After that, we headed to the Sugarloaf Marina for the ceremony. Talk about a beautiful spot! I love how the wedding party crosses over the bridge to get down close to the water for the ceremony. Once the ceremony finished, I took the wedding party around the marina for photos. The girls were drop-dead gorgeous, and the guys looked sharp. Everyone was laughing and having a great time.

Then it was down to just Chelsea and Adam. They're head over heels in love, and capturing that on camera was an absolute joy. They're like the perfect couple, and I'm so happy they found each other. You can see through the photos how happy they are together. After we wrapped up at the Sugarloaf Marina, we went to the Italian Hall in Port Colborne for the reception. The night finished up with delicious food, very heartwarming speeches and some fun dancing.


Congratulations to Chelsea and Adam on throwing one awesome wedding. It was a day to remember, and I'm honored to have been a part of it. Cheers to you both!
